Welcome to Chess Queen Club!

Chess Queen Club is an Online Chess Academy that runs as a Non-Profit Scheme, providing training on Chess Skills and Online Chess Tournaments at Competitive Levels to Kids and passionate Players. We offer a strong platform with tools for learning, practicing, and competing with other Tournament players. We provide the Best Coaching on the Chess Principles and practices to provide the finest skills and foundation to Kids.

Learn the Art of Chess through our Customized Online Classrooms, Quiz, Puzzles, notes, and Tournaments with Advanced Training Tools that will surely help you increase your Chess Levels.

Online Tournaments!

Enroll here for Free Rated OnBoard Chess Tournaments

We will conduct Free Chess Tournaments, Online and also Live on Board at the nearest Location in Charlotte City, North Carolina. Candidates 8+ years can enroll to play in our Chess Tournaments.
